The Akan name generator!

Hello there! This is a Javascript application that takes input of your year,month and date of birth then calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Being obedient to their culture to this date, children are given their first name as the one that corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Akan names are not just names given to children to give them identity. There is a specific traditional system that is followed when the naming is being done. The names also have different meanings and appellations. The popularity of Akan names is determined by their appellation. The more desirable the appellation is, the popular the name is. Curious find out what your Akan name is!!, proceed to filling the form below and clicking the generate name button.
