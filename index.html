Online Akan Name Generator

The Akan name generator!

Hello there! This is a Javascript application that takes input of your year,month and date of birth then calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender, outputs your Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Being obedient to their culture to this date, children are given their first name as the one that corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Curious find out what your Akan name is!!, proceed to filling the form below and clicking the generate name button.
