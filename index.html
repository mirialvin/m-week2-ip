AkanName

Ghanaian Name



Akan names are the day names in Ghana and are derived from Ghanaian culture.

In Ghana, the days of the week are very impotant aspects of the naming culture.



Babies are give a day name according to the day of the week they were born.

These day names come in pairs for each specific day; one for males and the other for females.
